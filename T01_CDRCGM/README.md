# T01_CDRCGM

This folder contains the first theory in my publication archive: `CDRCGM`, short for `Contractive Dynamical Reformulation of a Classical Geometric Method`.

## Article

- Main English source: `article/Contractive Dynamical Reformulation of a Classical Geometric Method for the Approximation of pi.tex`
- Associated PDF, when available: `article/Contractive Dynamical Reformulation of a Classical Geometric Method for the Approximation of pi.pdf`
- Zenodo record: `https://zenodo.org/records/19287090`

## Structure

- `article/`: manuscript source and editorial derivatives
- `experiments/`: scripts and protocols directly associated with the article
- `simulations/`: related simulations, when applicable
- `results/`: tables, summaries, and numerical artifacts associated with the theory

## Initial validation suite

This theory now includes the first executable validation seed of the archive:

- `experiments/t01_contraction_test.py`
- `simulations/t01_perturbation_decay.py`

These files start the broader program of building ten tests and ten simulations for each theory in the archive.

## Note

This theory is organized as the opening layer of the archive. The article already establishes the mathematical basis from which later stability-oriented theories emerge, especially the line that leads to `CPP` and, further ahead, to my optimization work.
