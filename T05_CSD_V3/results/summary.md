# CSD V3 Results Summary

This summary records the strongest compact evidence attached to the `V3` manuscript.

- Scenarios: 10
- Seeds per scenario: 16
- Cases: 160
- Mean temporal error: `V2 = 2.822e-01`, `V3 = 2.643e-01`
- Mean temporal gain of `V3`: `1.288x`
- Mean final error: `V2 = 6.439e-01`, `V3 = 6.118e-01`
- Mean final gain of `V3`: `1.205x`
- Temporal wins of `V3`: `160/160`
- Final wins of `V3`: `160/160`

## Observational validation

- Scenarios: 10
- Seeds per scenario: 8
- Cases: 80
- Mean temporal error: `V2 = 3.255e-01`
- Mean temporal error: `V3 quadratic = 3.118e-01`
- Mean temporal error: `V3 cubic = 2.185e-01`
- Gain of quadratic `V3` over `V2`: `1.072x`
- Gain of cubic `V3` over `V2`: `1.433x`
- Gain of cubic `V3` over quadratic `V3`: `1.348x`
- Quadratic wins over `V2`: `80/80`
- Cubic wins over `V2`: `80/80`
- Cubic wins over quadratic `V3`: `80/80`

## Interpretation

The current evidence supports `V3` as the coherent nonlinear single-chart regime beyond the affine theory. It improves systematically over `V2` in the benchmarked scenarios, and under degraded observational conditions the cubic nonlinear chart becomes decisively stronger than both the affine baseline and the quadratic `V3` proxy.
